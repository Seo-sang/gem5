Imbudo maintained peak winds for about 12 hours , before undergoing an eyewall replacement cycle . It weakened over land , but re @-@ intensified in the South China Sea , striking southern China near Yangjiang , Guangdong on July 24 . Imbudo rapidly weakened , dissipating on July 25 .
