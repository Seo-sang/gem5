 The center of education since the colonial period , Manila — particularly Intramuros — is home to several Philippine universities and colleges as well as its oldest ones . It served as the home of the University of Santo Tomas ( 1611 ) , Colegio de San Juan de <unk> ( 1620 ) , Ateneo de Manila University ( 1859 ) , Lyceum of the Philippines University and the <unk> Institute of Technology . The city is also the site of the Polytechnic University of the Philippines , the largest university in the country in terms of student population .
